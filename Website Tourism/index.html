How to get here

  1. From Manila, take the STAR Tollway towards Batangas City.
  2. Exit at Lipa or Batangas City, then follow signs to Mabini/Anilao.
  3. Public buses and vans (UV Express) also run regular routes — check schedules in advance.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to visit?

Dry season (November to May) is generally best for diving and island hopping. However, early weekdays can be quieter.

Are there guided hikes available?

Yes — local guides offer half-day and full-day hikes, often including sunrise or sunset options.

Is Mabini suitable for families?

Definitely — there are family-friendly resorts, calm beaches, and activities for all ages.
